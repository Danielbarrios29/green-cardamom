What is Green Cardamom?

Green cardamom, also known as true cardamom, is a spice derived from the seeds of the cardamom plant. It has a warm, sweet flavor with a strong aroma and is a favorite in Indian, Middle Eastern, and Scandinavian cuisine. In addition to taste the flavor in dishes, green cardamom is recognized for its health benefits, including digestive and blood circulation support, such as aiding digestion and being rich in antioxidants.

Green Cardamom Recipes

Green cardamom is recognized worldwide for its distinctive flavor, making it suitable for both sweet and savory dishes. This means it can be used in both desserts and main dishes. Some combinations include chai tea, rice pudding, curries, or baked goods like cookies and cakes. Grind the seeds for optimal aroma or add the whole pods to soups and stews.

Health Benefits of Green Cardamom

Green cardamom contains high levels of antioxidants and may help reduce stomach inflammation and gas. It can aid digestion, freshen breath, and promote cardiovascular health. Some studies indicate that it may help control blood sugar and fight bacteria in the body.
